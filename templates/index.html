    Welcome to revive

    Nutrition is a natural way of improving your full body health

    Poor health can control your life. Although modern medicine is an incredible help, medical professionals are not available, and especially in the kidney industry, many nutritionists have a backlog of patients to see. Because those with kidney damage have a limited amount of time to respond to health concerns, we created Revive.

    Revive is a website that provides you with AI food recommendations catered towards your dietary needs. Whether you are in between appointments or just trying to have nutritional autonomy, Revive has you in mind.

    How it works?

    Sign up

    Creating an account takes just a few short minutes. We collect information about your health to provide the best artifical intelligence recommendations catered towards your needs.

    Log meals

    Writing down your daily meal intake keeps you and your nutritionist aware of what specific foods, times, or serving sizes may be imbalancing your nutritional health.

    Observe dashboards

    Our sophiscated software calculates from your meals what nutrients you are within your recommended personal range for and which nutrients can use some improving on.

    Choose your adventure

    Using food suggestions specifically for your individual needs, find what you like best! Our recommendations update in real time as you update your meal journal.
